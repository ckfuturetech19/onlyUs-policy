Privacy Policy
Last Updated: January 2025

üíù Your Privacy Matters to Us

OnlyUs is a one-to-one chat app designed exclusively for couples to protect your intimate conversations. No group chats, no social features, no distractions - just you and your partner. This policy explains how we handle your personal information while keeping your relationship completely private and secure.

1. Information We Collect

Account & Identity Information:

  • Email address (for account creation and verification)
  • Age verification (18+ confirmation required)
  • Display name (chosen by you)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Couple ID (unique identifier for your relationship)
  • Device information (for security and authentication)

Communication & Activity Data:

  • One-to-one private messages between you and your partner only (encrypted)
  • Photos, videos, and media files shared in your private chat (encrypted)
  • Voice messages and private calls between you two (encrypted)
  • Online status and last seen information (visible only to your partner)
  • Message delivery and read status (private between you and your partner)
  • Login times and session data (for security purposes only)

Note: OnlyUs only supports one-to-one conversations. We never collect group chat data, social media interactions, or any communication outside your private couple chat.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Verify you meet our 18+ age requirement
  • Enable secure one-to-one messaging between you and your partner only
  • Sync your private conversations across your devices
  • Provide presence indicators (online/offline status visible only to your partner)
  • Deliver push notifications for new messages from your partner
  • Maintain your private chat room security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Provide technical support for your one-to-one chat experience
  • Improve app performance and security features for couple communication

What we DON'T do: Create social networks, enable group chats, connect you with other users, or facilitate any communication outside your one-to-one relationship.

üîí Zero-Knowledge Privacy

We operate on a zero-knowledge principle - we cannot and do not read, analyze, or access your one-to-one messages. Your private conversations are encrypted with keys that only exist on your and your partner's devices. Even if compelled by law enforcement, we cannot provide message content because we don't have access to it. OnlyUs staff cannot see your conversations, and no other users can ever access your private chat room.

3. Advanced Security & Data Protection

OnlyUs employs enterprise-grade security measures to protect your intimate conversations:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All messages are encrypted before leaving your device
  • Firebase Security Rules: Strict database access controls ensure only authorized users can access data
  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted in transit
  • Regular Security Audits: Continuous monitoring and security assessments
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Optional additional security layer for account protection
  • Secure File Storage: Media files are encrypted and stored with restricted access
  • Session Management: Automatic logout and session timeout for inactive accounts
  • Device Authentication: Each device is verified before accessing your account

Your conversations are protected with the same level of security used by banks and government institutions. Even OnlyUs staff cannot access your messages - they're encrypted with keys only you and your partner possess.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We maintain a strict no-sharing policy for your one-to-one conversations. Your personal information and private chat data are never sold, rented, or shared with third parties. Limited access may occur only in these scenarios:

  • You and your partner only: Your conversations are visible only to you two - no other users can access your private chat
  • Technical support: With explicit permission and only for troubleshooting your one-to-one chat issues
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, we'll notify you when legally permitted (note: encrypted messages cannot be decrypted even if requested)
  • Safety concerns: Only aggregated, non-identifiable data for preventing abuse in the platform

We never work with: Advertisers, data brokers, social media companies, dating platforms, or any third parties seeking user data for commercial purposes. OnlyUs will never become a social network or enable connections beyond your one partner.

5. Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your one-to-one chat experience:

  • Access your personal data and chat history with your partner
  • Delete your account and all associated private conversation data
  • Export your one-to-one chat history
  • Update your information anytime
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Block or disconnect from your partner if needed
  • Control your online status visibility to your partner

Important: Since OnlyUs is designed for one-to-one communication, deleting your account will also affect your partner's access to your shared conversation history.

6. Data Retention

We keep your one-to-one chat data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • All private messages with your partner are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Personal information is removed immediately
  • Your partner will be notified that the shared conversation history will be affected
  • Some technical logs may be retained for security purposes (max 90 days, no message content)

Note: Since OnlyUs facilitates one-to-one conversations, both partners share the same conversation history. Account deletion affects the shared chat room for both users.

7. Cookies & Analytics

OnlyUs uses minimal tracking:

  • Essential cookies for app functionality
  • Basic crash reporting to improve app stability
  • No advertising or behavioral tracking

8. Age Requirement & Privacy

OnlyUs is exclusively for users 18 years and older. We implement strict age verification and don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. Users must confirm their age during registration. If you're a parent and believe someone under 18 has created an account, please contact us immediately for account removal.

9. International Users

OnlyUs is available globally. Your data may be stored in servers located in different countries, but always with the same high level of protection regardless of location.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do:

  • You'll be notified in the app
  • The updated policy will be posted here
  • Continued use means you accept the changes
